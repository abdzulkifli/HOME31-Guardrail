# HOME31-Guardrail
HOME31-Guardrail is a strategic governance and decision-support framework designed to ensure that all HOME31 initiatives, investments and policy decisions remain aligned with LPPSA’s mandate, risk appetite, financial sustainability and long-term strategic direction.

It provides a structured set of checks, thresholds and decision principles to help management assess whether a proposed initiative is strategically aligned, financially responsible, operationally feasible, digitally ready and supported by appropriate governance and assurance mechanisms.

The framework enables LPPSA to pursue transformation and innovation without compromising institutional resilience, regulatory obligations, customer trust or organisational accountability.

HOME31-Guardrail supports:

early identification of strategic, financial, operational and governance risks;
consistent evaluation of initiatives across HOME31 pillars;
clearer trade-offs between ambition, affordability, risk and execution capability;
stronger accountability through defined ownership, thresholds and escalation points;
evidence-based decision-making using scenarios, indicators and measurable outcomes; and
continuous monitoring to ensure initiatives remain within approved strategic boundaries.

As an evergreen framework, HOME31-Guardrail will be reviewed and updated over time to reflect changes in strategy, regulation, risk exposure, technology, financial conditions and organisational priorities.

Its purpose is not to slow transformation, but to ensure that transformation moves forward with discipline, confidence and control.
